Working Papers

Garel, A., Michaely, R., and Romec, A. Climate Disclosure and Director Elections.
R&R · Management Science
Firms not disclosing carbon emissions receive significantly lower support for their directors in board elections. Firms initiating climate disclosure see increased director support. Sustainable funds and universal investors are key drivers of this trend.
Climate Disclosure Director Elections ESG

Di Giuli, A., Garel, A., and Romec, A. The Voting Behavior of Women-Led Mutual Funds. ECGI Working Paper.
R&R · Journal of Corporate Finance
Women-led mutual funds are more likely to support environmental and social (ES) proposals, but not governance ones. Women-led mutual funds are also more likely to vote with management in firms headed by female CEOs.
Gender Mutual Funds Voting ESG

Bourveau, T., Garel, A., and Romec, A. Mandatory Carbon Disclosure: Evidence from France.
R&R · European Accounting Review
We examine firms' response to a carbon disclosure mandate imposed on French firms with more than 500 employees by the Grenelle II law. Only half of the firms subject to the mandate comply and file at least one carbon report between 2014 and 2021.
Carbon Disclosure Climate Policy France

Derrien, F., Garel, A., Romec, A., and Zhou, F. Climate Risk Engagements.
Working Paper
Climate risk engagements represent a growing fraction of ESG engagements by large institutional investors and are more frequent in high carbon emissions industries. Following engagement, targeted firms are more likely to commit to science-based climate targets and to disclose climate-related information.
Climate Risk Engagement ESG

De Bock, K., & Garel, A. From Vision to Prediction: A Multimodal-AI Pipeline to Generate Perceptual Attributes for Price Prediction.
Working Paper
We develop a multimodal AI pipeline that combines computer vision and language models to generate perceptual attributes from images and text, and use them to predict prices. The approach demonstrates that AI-generated perceptual features contain economically meaningful information beyond traditional quantitative variables.
Artificial Intelligence Machine Learning Computer Vision Price Prediction

Garel, A., Cheng, E. Minimum Energy Performance Standards and Housing Price Capitalization: Evidence from France's Rental Ban on 'Energy Sieves'.
Working Paper
Using more than one million housing transactions over 2014–2024, we document that energy sieves — the least energy-efficient houses subject to France's 2021 progressive rental ban — sell at an additional discount of about 9 percentage points following the policy announcement.
Energy Performance Housing Prices Climate Policy
