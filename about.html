About

Welcome to my personal website. I am currently a Full Professor in Finance at Audencia Business School and Affiliated Researcher at the HKU Sustainability Research Institute. My research focuses on corporate finance, behavioral finance, climate finance, and biodiversity finance. I am particularly interested in the voting behavior of ESG funds, the ESG engagement of institutional investors, biodiversity dependence and transition risks, and the lack of market discipline on climate commitments.

I served as scientific advisor for several finance-related projects in the industry. I currently serve on the scientific committee of a consortium of French institutions initiating two large biodiversity funds. I am also a qualified expert for the FIR (French Forum for Sustainable and Responsible Investment).
