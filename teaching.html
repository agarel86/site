Post-Graduate · Audencia Business School
Sustainable Investing
Covers the theory and practice of sustainable and responsible investing: measurement of the sustainable profile of a company, ESG investment strategies, exit versus voice, performance of ESG investing, real effects of ESG investment, greenwashing, climate funds, biodiversity funds, and human capital funds.
ESG Climate Finance Biodiversity Greenwashing Portfolio Strategies
Post-Graduate · Audencia Business School
Machine Learning for Finance
Hands-on introduction to Machine Learning, Neural Networks, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Artificial Intelligence applied to finance using Python. Students learn to implement predictive models, natural language processing, and data-driven investment strategies.
Machine Learning Neural Networks LLM Python AI in Finance
Post-Graduate · Audencia Business School
Collecting and Analyzing Finance Data from LSEG with Python
Basics of data collection from LSEG Workspace with Python for finance data analysis. Students learn to connect to the LSEG API, retrieve market and fundamental data, and perform exploratory financial data analysis using Python.
Python LSEG Workspace Data Collection Finance Data API
