Review of Finance · 30(1) · pp. 231–272
Garel, A., Romec, A., Sautner, Z., & Wagner, A. F. Firm-Level Nature Dependence.
BiodiversityNature RiskESG
+
We construct firm-level measures of dependence on ecosystem services (NatureDep scores) for 31,772 listed firms in 117 countries between 2010 and 2023. NatureDep scores positively correlate with impact on biodiversity, are unrelated to nature-related actions disclosed in the CDP survey, are not reflected in firms' corporate disclosures, and predict BlackRock's biodiversity-related engagements. The scores relate positively to measures of downside risk, and predict nature-related incidents.
